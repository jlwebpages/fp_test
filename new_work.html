Note to Self

Malibu

12 x 12 inch wood panel set in a maple floater frame

The hills of Malibu are known for their stunning views - but in December 2024 the Franklin Fire reminded us how precarious those views can be. Unfortunately, more devastating fires hit the same area less than a month later. The land is beginning to recover and the beauty has begun the long process of recovery.
