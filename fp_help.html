STANDINGS:

The STANDINGS page shows the most recent scores, season average scores, and season winnings for each player.

FORMS:  Input Form

Use the Input Form to submit your picks. Go to the FORMS page and click Input Form. Enter the password and click Submit to display the Input Form. When you hover the mouse over any Input Form button, a pop-up box will appear explaining what happens when the button is clicked. Fill out the Input Form starting with your name, then click Accept. Review your picks, then click Submit via E-Mail. A new e-mail message containing your picks will be displayed. Click Send to e-mail your picks to me. If a new e-mail message isn't displayed, click Copy to E-Mail and follow the instructions to submit your picks.

Tip:  After you send me your picks, if you don't get an e-mail response indicating I Got 'em, then I probably didn't receive your picks and you should resend them and/or contact me. Otherwise, you could get a zero for the week.

FORMS:  Picks

Go to the FORMS page and click Picks. When you enter in game winners (regular season) or game scores (post season) and click Calculate Player Scores, the Picks form displays each player's score so you can see who's winning. The Picks form will retrieve and display NFL game scores from the internet and calculate player scores when you click Get NFL Scores. To automatically refresh scores, click Auto Refresh: Start.

When you click Best Outcome on the regular season Picks form, games with winners already specified ("H", "V", or "Tie") will remain unchanged. Each of the other games will be set to "H" or "V" depending on what yields the best outcome for the selected Player. The best outcome is when the selected player ends up first with the biggest difference between his score and the second place score. If it's not possible for the selected player to end up first, then the best outcome is when the difference between his score and the first place score is smallest. If an Opponent is also selected, then the best outcome is when the selected player's score is higher than the opponent's score and the difference between their scores is biggest. If it's not possible for the selected player's score to be higher than the opponent's score, then the best outcome is when the difference between their scores is smallest.

FORMS:  Results

Go to the FORMS page and click Results. The Results form shows the most recent NFL game results and player scores. Prior week NFL game results and player scores can be accessed by using the pull-down menu.

FORMS:  Season Summary

Go to the FORMS page and click Season Summary. The Season Summary form shows player rankings, games won, and scores for all regular season weeks completed.

ARCHIVE:

The ARCHIVE page allows access to past season results and standings.
