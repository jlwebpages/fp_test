HOME:

When AFC and NFC playoff teams are displayed on the desktop version of the HOME page, and you hover the mouse over any team logo, the team name will appear in a tooltip message. If the team is winning one or more tie breaks, information about the tie break(s) will also appear in the message.

STANDINGS:

The STANDINGS page shows player rankings and scores for the most recent week, season total scores, and season winnings.

FORMS:  Input Form

Use the Input Form to submit your picks. Go to the FORMS page and click Input Form, then enter the password and click Submit. When you hover the mouse over any Input Form button, information about what happens when you click the button appears in a tooltip message. Fill out the Input Form starting with your name, then click Accept. Review your picks, then click Submit via E-Mail. A new e-mail message containing your picks will be displayed. Click Send to e-mail your picks to me. If a new e-mail message isn't displayed, click Copy to E-Mail and follow the instructions to submit your picks.

Tip:  After you send me your picks, if you don't get an e-mail response indicating I Got 'em, then I probably didn't receive your picks and you should resend them and/or contact me. Otherwise, you could get a zero for the week.

FORMS:  Picks

Go to the FORMS page and click Picks. When you enter in game winners (regular season) or game scores (postseason) and click Calculate Player Scores, scores will be calculated so you can see who's winning. When you click Get NFL Scores, NFL game scores will be retrieved from the internet and player scores will be calculated. To automatically refresh NFL game scores, click Auto Refresh: Start.

Clicking Best Outcome on the regular season form will figure out the best possible outcome for the selected Player. Games with winners already specified (H, V, or Tie) will remain unchanged and the other games will be set to H or V to yield the best outcome. The best outcome is when the selected Player is first and the difference between his score and the second place score is maximized. If that's not possible, then the best outcome is when the difference between selected Player's score and the first place score is minimized. If an Opponent is selected, then best outcome is relative to the selected Opponent.

FORMS:  Results

Go to the FORMS page and click Results. The Results form shows the most recent NFL game results and player scores. On the regular season form, prior week NFL game results and player scores can be accessed using the pull-down menu.

FORMS:  Summary

Go to the FORMS page and click Summary. The Summary form shows player rankings, scores, and games won for all regular season weeks. It also shows player rankings for regular season total scores and shows regular season total games won.

ARCHIVE:

The ARCHIVE page provides access to past season results and standings, and past season AFC and NFC playoff teams.
